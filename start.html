Welcome to the Card Game!

Click "Start" to begin the game. Guess if the next card will be higher or lower than the current card.
Press the "Higher" button if you think the next card will be higher, or the "Lower" button if you think it will be lower.
You can include Jokers in the deck by checking the box below.
The game will keep track of your score and display a message after each guess.

Good luck!
