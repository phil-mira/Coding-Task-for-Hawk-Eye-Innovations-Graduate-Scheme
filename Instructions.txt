Higher or Lower Card Game Instructions

Objective:
The objective of the game is to correctly guess whether the next card drawn from the deck will be higher or lower than the current card.

Setup:
1. A standard deck of 52 playing cards is used.
2. Shuffle the deck thoroughly before starting the game.

Gameplay:
1. Draw the first card from the deck and place it face up.
2. The player must guess whether the next card drawn will be higher or lower than the current card.
3. Draw the next card from the deck and place it face up.
4. If the player's guess is correct, they score a point and continue to guess for the next card.
5. If the player's guess is incorrect, the game ends and their score is recorded.
6. Repeat steps 2-5 until the player makes an incorrect guess or the deck is exhausted.

Card Values:
- Cards are ranked from 2 (lowest) to Ace (highest).
- Suits do not affect the ranking of the cards.

Special Rules:
- If the current card and the next card are of the same rank, the player loses the game.
- The game can be played with multiple players taking turns or as a single-player game.

Winning:
- The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins.
- In a single-player game, the objective is to achieve the highest possible score.

Enjoy the game and good luck!
